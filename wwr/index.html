Expeditions to many rivers

Since 1953, Whitewater company has run world class rafting trips on all USA. We have many packages according to every budget.

Reconnect with nature on breathtaking river adventures across the region.

  • Multi‑day rafting journeys through pristine canyons, lush forests, and remote wilderness areas.
  • Rapids ranging from gentle Class I/II family‑friendly sections to exhilarating Class III/IV waves for thrill‑seekers.
  • Crystal‑clear river corridors flowing beneath towering cliffs, ancient rock formations, and scenic wildlife habitat.
  • Relax in riverside camps with comfortable tents, star‑filled skies, and optional natural hot springs depending on the location.
  • Enjoy chef‑prepared meals featuring fresh, local ingredients. Vegetarian, vegan, and gluten‑free options available.
